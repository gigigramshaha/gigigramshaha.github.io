Who are you?

Georgeann Martin. Imagine that.

I can tell you who I'm not: I'm not the brainiac who could code before they could walk, I'm not a member of the esteemed high IQ society Mensa, and somehow I don't think I'll be the first woman on the moon. Bummer.

Even still, I'm unlike anyone you've ever met. I have―get this―varied interests. Pretty unique, right? The culture of seeking jobs or figuring out your life's pursuit seems to have shifted towards forcing people to decide on one, maybe two, interests and excelling purely at that. But I don't think most people can fit in one box. I know I don't.


What's this website?

I took one course on front-end development, and here we are.

Back in the Fall of 2020, I took a software engineering course as a part of my degree program. Students were divided up into teams to create different components for a website, and the end result was so satisfying, I had to find some excuse to do it again.

This website serves as a journal for my interests, whether it's a project I completed, a review for a book I read, or a blog on why Game of Thrones has been bad since season 5. Look around, enjoy your stay, and feel free to steal some ideas!
